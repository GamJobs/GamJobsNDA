Section 1

Definition of Confidential Information

For purposes of this Agreement, "Confidential Information" means any and all non-public information disclosed — directly or indirectly, in writing, orally, electronically, or by any other means — by GAMJOBS.io to the Receiving Party, including but not limited to:

  • Business plans, strategies, growth roadmaps, and market analyses

  • Partnership agreements, vendor relationships, and investor details

Confidential Information does not include information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no breach of this Agreement; (b) was already known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure; or (c) is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of any Confidential Information.

Section 2

Obligations of the Receiving Party

The Receiving Party agrees to:

  • Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence and treat it with at least the same degree of care used to protect their own confidential information (but no less than reasonable care)

  • Limit access to Confidential Information to individuals with a clear need to know, who are bound by substantially similar obligations
  • Promptly notify GAMJOBS.io in writing of any unauthorized disclosure, misuse, or suspected breach of this Agreement

Section 3

Restrictions – Prohibited Activities

Without the prior written consent of GAMJOBS.io, the Receiving Party shall not:

  • Share or disclose any Confidential Information to third parties, competitors, media, or the public in any form
